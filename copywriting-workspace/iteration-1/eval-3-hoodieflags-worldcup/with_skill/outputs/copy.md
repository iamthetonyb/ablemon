## Metadata

**Framework:** AIDA
- *Attention:* Specificity as a hook — "97 days out" is concrete, not vague urgency. Immediately frames the divide between planners and scramblers.
- *Interest:* Product proof before price. "Serious fan" framing positions the hoodie as a quality gear decision, not an impulse buy.
- *Desire:* Three concrete pre-order advantages stacked without repetition. Closes with the calculus Orange readers respond to ("the math is straightforward").
- *Action:* Scarcity-anchored CTA. "Before your country sells out" makes inaction feel costly.

**Meta-Programs Deployed:**
- *Motivation Direction:* Toward (primary) — "show up ready," "lock in," "gear up smart," "move now." Toward language matches the Orange achiever profile; these buyers are planning to win, not just avoid looking bad.
- *Frame of Reference:* Internal — no external authority or social proof pressure. The reader is positioned as the one who knows what serious fans do. CTA assumes the decision is theirs to make ("lock in your pre-order").
- *Sorting Filter:* Difference — positions HoodieFlags against generic fan merch ("this isn't a novelty item"). Orange readers want to buy the thing that's actually better, not the default.
- *Chunk Size:* Global (opener) → Specific (97 days, $49.99, $75 threshold, 30+ countries) — grounds abstract urgency in concrete numbers.
- *Decision Factor:* Options-oriented — stacks three pre-order advantages so the buyer feels they're choosing the smart package, not just a hoodie.

**Spiral Dynamics Level:** 5 — Orange/Achiever
- Language: "smart," "straightforward," "plan ahead," "serious fan," "move now," "show up ready"
- Avoids: tribal/blue language ("unity," "community"), green language ("express yourself," "celebrate diversity"), or red/impulsive language
- Orange appeals to strategic self-interest: the value math is presented explicitly, not emotionally

**Representational System:** Visual — "show up looking the part," "full-coverage flag designs," "holds color," "show up ready"

**Urgency Mechanism:** Countdown specificity ("97 days") + stock scarcity ("before your country sells out") + logical deadline framing (guaranteed delivery before kickoff)
