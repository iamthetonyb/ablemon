Date: 2026-03-07

## Critical / High Impact (<15 min each)

1. Lock Phase 1 landing page to a single URL
- Set all prospecting destinations to: `https://hoodieflags.com/collections/country-flags-2nd-gen`
- Prevents LP variable contamination during creative test.

2. Append frame-style metadata to TikTok UTMs
- Add `utm_term=crop_short22` or `utm_term=padded_short22`.
- Keeps frame-style test measurable in GA4.

3. Enforce variant-level `utm_content`
- Use `utm_content=V04` style values on every TikTok ad.
- Prevents variant attribution blending.

4. Keep TikTok objective fallback rule explicit
- If purchase volume is thin, optimize to ATC in early phase.
- Prevents learning stagnation at low spend.

5. Confirm bulk discount visibility on mobile above fold
- Quick QA on collection + top PDPs.
- Aligns ad promise with immediate on-page evidence.

6. Freeze non-essential edits for first 72 hours
- No audience/creative/LP structural edits during initial signal collection.
- Reduces noisy resets.

7. Use only winner frame style after Day 2
- Drop losing frame style quickly once signal appears.
- Reclaims budget for higher-probability variants.

8. Pre-stage patent-safe fallback copy
- Keep claim-neutral backup headline/body ready in ad manager.
- Speeds response if policy review flags patent language.

9. Verify event mapping in GA4 and ad platforms
- Validate `view_item`, `add_to_cart`, `begin_checkout`, `purchase` from test clicks.
- Protects reporting trust before spend ramps.

10. Keep stop rules visible in dashboard notes
- Pause candidate when spend > $15 and CTR < 0.5% with no ATC.
- Enforces discipline under constrained budget.
